1. Program
----------

**Monday**

14:00 Welcome message, etc... Leveque, Roche |br|
14:05 **Brachet**: *Gross-Pitaevskii description of superfluid dynamics at finite-temperature: a short review of recent result*s |br|
14:35 **Salort**: *Continuous 2 fluid model with a circulation-quantum-consistent truncation* |br|
15:05 **Baggaley**: *Tree methods, and the detection of vortical structures in the vortex filament method* |br|
15:35 (Coffee break) |br|
15:50 Round table: *Beyond the limitations of the 3 traditional models of superfluid turbulence* - Facilitator: Brachet |br|
17:00 (End) 

**Tuesday**

09:00 **Danaila**: *Advanced numerical methods for computing vortex states in Bose-Einstein condensates* |br|
09:30 **Kerr**: *Time scaling and dynamics near GP reconnection events* |br|
10:00 **Salman**: *Simulating Vortex Dynamics with the Gross-Pitaevskii Equation* |br|
10:30 (Coffee break) |br|
10:50 Round table: *New numerical approach / Challenge on the numerical sid*e - Facilitator: Kerr |br|
12:00 (Lunch) 

14:00 **Barenghi**: *Properties of homogeneous isotropic quantum turbulence* |br|
14:30 **Sergeev**: *Time-dependent spectra in zero-temperature limit. Reconnections, anisotropy, and (perhaps) a 3D inverse cascade* |br|
15:00 **Skrbek**: *Recent Prague second sound and visualization experiments as a source for simulations QT studies* |br|
15:30 (Coffee break) |br|
15:50 Round table: *Open problems waiting for numerical insight (KW cascade excluded*) - Facilitator: Barenghi |br|
17:00 (End) 

**Wednesday**

09:00 **Hanninen**: *Kelvin waves: identification and dynamics* |br|
09:30 **Krstulovic**: *Kelvin-wave cascade and dissipation in low-temperature superfluids vortices* |br|
10:00 **Proment**: *Kelvin wave interactions and decay in the Gross-Pitaevskii model* |br|
10:30 (Coffee break) |br|
10:50 **Laurie**: *The nonlocality of the Kelvin-wave energy transfer* |br|
11:20 Round table: *Kelvin wave cascade at 0K* - Facilitator: Hanninen |br|
12:30 (Lunch and end)
