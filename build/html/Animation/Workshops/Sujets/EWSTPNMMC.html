European Workshop on Superfluid turbulence from the perspective of numerics: modeling, methods and challenges¶

Salle de réunion LR6 C 023, Centre Blaise Pascal, ENS-Lyon, France
November 26-28, 2012

Organizing committee:

  • Emmanuel Lévêque, Laboratoire de Physique, Ecole Normale Supérieure de Lyon, France

  • Philippe-Emmanuel Roche, Institut Neel, Grenoble, France

  • Laurent Chevillard, Laboratoire de Physique, Ecole Normale Supérieure de Lyon, France

  • Bernard Castaing, Laboratoire de Physique, Ecole Normale Supérieure de Lyon, France

Administrative coordination:

  • Samantha Barendson, CBP, ENS de Lyon, France (samantha.barendson @ ens-lyon.fr)

The workshop is supported by:

  • the Physics Laboratory at the ENS de Lyon

  • the Centre Blaise Pascal

1. Program¶

Monday

14:00 Welcome message, etc… Leveque, Roche
14:05 Brachet: Gross-Pitaevskii description of superfluid dynamics at finite-temperature: a short review of recent result*s |br| 14:35 **Salort*: Continuous 2 fluid model with a circulation-quantum-consistent truncation
15:05 Baggaley: Tree methods, and the detection of vortical structures in the vortex filament method
15:35 (Coffee break)
15:50 Round table: Beyond the limitations of the 3 traditional models of superfluid turbulence - Facilitator: Brachet
17:00 (End)

Tuesday

09:00 Danaila: Advanced numerical methods for computing vortex states in Bose-Einstein condensates
09:30 Kerr: Time scaling and dynamics near GP reconnection events
10:00 Salman: Simulating Vortex Dynamics with the Gross-Pitaevskii Equation
10:30 (Coffee break)
10:50 Round table: *New numerical approach / Challenge on the numerical sid*e - Facilitator: Kerr
12:00 (Lunch)

14:00 Barenghi: Properties of homogeneous isotropic quantum turbulence
14:30 Sergeev: Time-dependent spectra in zero-temperature limit. Reconnections, anisotropy, and (perhaps) a 3D inverse cascade
15:00 Skrbek: Recent Prague second sound and visualization experiments as a source for simulations QT studies
15:30 (Coffee break)
15:50 Round table: Open problems waiting for numerical insight (KW cascade excluded) - Facilitator: Barenghi
17:00 (End)

Wednesday

09:00 Hanninen: Kelvin waves: identification and dynamics
09:30 Krstulovic: Kelvin-wave cascade and dissipation in low-temperature superfluids vortices
10:00 Proment: Kelvin wave interactions and decay in the Gross-Pitaevskii model
10:30 (Coffee break)
10:50 Laurie: The nonlocality of the Kelvin-wave energy transfer
11:20 Round table: Kelvin wave cascade at 0K - Facilitator: Hanninen
12:30 (Lunch and end)
